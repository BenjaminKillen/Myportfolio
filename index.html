About Me

Cloud Infrastructure & DevOps Specialist

I'm a DevOps Engineer specializing in cloud infrastructure automation, CI/CD pipelines, and container orchestration. I design and implement robust deployment pipelines that enable organizations to deliver software faster, more reliably, and at scale.

With deep expertise in Infrastructure as Code, Kubernetes, and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP), I help teams automate their entire software delivery lifecycle. I'm passionate about building resilient infrastructure, implementing GitOps workflows, managing containerized environments, and optimizing cloud costs. My work focuses on creating self-healing systems, implementing monitoring and observability, and ensuring high availability and disaster recovery across cloud environments.
